William Shakespeare

The Bard of Avon

Playwright Extraordinaire

Shakespeare wrote 39 plays, including tragedies like Hamlet and Macbeth, comedies like A Midsummer Night's Dream, and histories like Henry V.

Master of Sonnets

He composed 154 sonnets that explore themes of love, beauty, politics, and mortality, with his unique rhyming scheme.

Inventor of Words

Shakespeare introduced over 1,700 words to the English language, including "eyeball," "fashionable," and "lonely."

Globe Theatre

His plays were performed at the Globe Theatre in London, which was reconstructed in 1997 and still hosts performances today.

Lasting Legacy

Shakespeare's works have been translated into every major living language and are performed more often than any other playwright.
